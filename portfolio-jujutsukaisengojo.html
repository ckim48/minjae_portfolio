Drawing from Jujutsu Kaisen

This is also a drawing from Jujutsu Kaisen. The character is Gojo Satoru, and same as other drawings, the process involved rough sketch first and moved on to further work on details and cleaning up. However, I feel like this drawing is not done that good, because the lines seem messy, and the colors do not fit together that well. The shadows for both the face and the clothes are not done cleanly. This motivated me to be improve my skills and work on drawings with more patience and concentration.
